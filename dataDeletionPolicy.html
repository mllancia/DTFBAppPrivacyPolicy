Data Deletion Policy for API Portal

Users have the right to request the deletion of their personal data collected by API Portal. To request data deletion, please follow these steps:

Email Request

Send an email to [Your Contact Email] with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

Details

In your email, please provide the following information:

  • Your Instagram username
  • The email associated with your account
  • A brief description of your request

Upon receiving your request, we will verify your identity and proceed with deleting your data from our system within 15 days.
